About Us

Welcome to Mama Njie's Restaurant, where we bring the flavors of The Gambia to your table. Our restaurant is built on a simple foundation - serving authentic Gambian food prepared with love and fresh ingredients.

Every dish we serve tells a story. Our recipes come from family traditions passed down through generations, prepared the way they've been made for years. We don't rush our cooking. Whether it's a slow-cooked Domoda or perfectly seasoned Benachin, each meal is made with care and time.

We believe the best meals bring people together. Whether you're joining us with family, friends, or new people, our restaurant is a place where everyone feels welcome. We serve authentic Gambian hospitality - warm, genuine, and real.

Located on Jalangbam highway, Mama Njie's is more than just a restaurant - it's a gathering place where you can experience true Gambian culture through food. Come hungry, leave happy.
